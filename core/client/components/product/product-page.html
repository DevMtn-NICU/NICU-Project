Connecting Hospitals, Patients, Family, and Friends

Share Heart 2 Heart is an interactive service for hospitals to create family-centered care by connecting patients in isolated medical conditions to family and friends.


It is a web portal that creates a virtual support community for the patient and caregivers.


Originated by the mother of a NICU child, this application has been designed to connect the hospital to the caregiver and the caregiver to the community.


Possible health situations could include the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit, Burn Unit, Intensive Care Unit, and Hospice Care.
